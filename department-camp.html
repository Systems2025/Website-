Our Core Beliefs

At Camp BlueSky, We Believe In Life-Giving Relationships

Our activities help campers embrace the adventure of learning new skills and conquering new challenges. We design each activity to be an adventure that builds relationships with peers, staff, and the source of ALL adventure, Jesus.

Grow Spiritually

Our aim is for students to know the love of Jesus and grow deeper in relationship with Him.

Build Friendships

Our programs exist to help students develop deeper relationships with their peers as well as create new friendships.

Have Fun!

Our hope is for our programs to reflect the joy and adventure of following Christ.

Meet Our Camp Staff

Our Camp BlueSky Staff is made up of university student volunteers of different nationalities and backgrounds who have experience working with young people.

Our staff members go through an extensive application, interview, and training process to best serve every camper. Each Camp BlueSky staff member is a follower of Jesus Christ who will strive to model Christ-like behavior for all campers and care for them right where they are.
